## Full description
```text
Quietly is a calm, private way to save public media you have the right to keep.

Paste a direct link to a public image or video, and Quietly checks that it’s a
real, publicly available media file — then saves it neatly to your gallery. No
clutter, no noise, no sign-up. Just the file you wanted, saved.

Quietly is built on a simple principle: save only what is public and what you
have the rights or permission to save.

WHAT QUIETLY DOES
• Saves direct, public image and video links to your device gallery
• Checks a link before saving, so you know what you’re getting
• Keeps a tidy history of what you’ve saved, with quick open and share
• Works calmly and predictably, with clear, honest messages

WHAT QUIETLY DOES NOT DO
• It does not scrape websites or social platforms
• It does not access private, login-only, or protected content
• It does not bypass any protection or sign-in
• It has no account, no ads, and no tracking

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No advertising and no advertising ID
• No analytics, no tracking, no profiling
• No account and no sign-in
• Your settings and history stay on your device — nothing is sent to us
Read our privacy policy: https://uselunexa.com/privacy/quietly

CALM, CAREFUL, HONEST
Quietly is intentionally small and respectful. When a link can’t be saved —
because it isn’t public, isn’t a direct media file, or can’t be reached —
Quietly tells you plainly instead of guessing.

ACCEPTABLE USE
Quietly is for saving media that is publicly available and that you have the
right to save — for example your own content, or content you have permission to
use. Please respect creators’ rights and the terms of the sites you use.

Made by Lunexa.
Questions or feedback: hello@uselunexa.com
```
