# Privacy Policy — Quietly

**Last updated: 29 May 2026**

Quietly ("the app") is a calm, rights-aware media saver published by
**Lunexa** ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data the app does
and does not handle. In short: **Quietly does not collect, transmit, or sell
any personal data. Everything the app stores stays on your device.**

## Who we are
Quietly is published by Lunexa. For any privacy question or request, contact us
at **hello@uselunexa.com**.
[Data controller: Lunexa — to be completed with the registered legal entity /
responsible person and address as required by GDPR (Art. 13) and KVKK (Md. 10).]

## What Quietly does
Quietly lets you save **direct, publicly accessible media files** (images and
videos) from links you choose to paste into the app. It then stores those files
on your device and in your device gallery. Quietly does not browse social
platforms, does not scrape web pages, and does not access private, login-only,
or protected content.

## Data we collect
**None.** We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our
servers. Quietly has:
- no user accounts or sign-in,
- no advertising and no advertising identifier,
- no analytics, tracking, or profiling,
- no crash-reporting or third-party data SDKs.

## Data stored on your device
The following stays **only on your device** and is never sent to us:
- **Preferences** — your selected quality and a few simple on/off settings.
- **Save history** — for each item you save: the link you pasted and its host,
  the media type and size, the local file path, and a timestamp.
- **Saved media files** — the images/videos you choose to save, kept in the
  app's storage and added to your device gallery.

You can delete this data at any time (see "Your choices and deletion").

## Network requests
Quietly makes network requests only in two situations, and **neither sends your
personal data to us**:
1. **When you paste a link and save media** — the app contacts the server of the
   URL **you** provided to check and download the file. That request is between
   you and that third-party server, which may receive standard technical
   information (such as your IP address and request headers) and is governed by
   **that server's own privacy policy**. We are not involved and receive nothing.
2. **Connectivity check** — to know whether you are online, the app sends a
   small request to a standard Google endpoint (`www.gstatic.com`). This request
   contains **no personal data** and no information about what you save.

## Permissions
- **Internet** — to check and download the media file from the link you provide.
- **Save to your photos/gallery** — to write the media you choose into your
  device gallery. Quietly only **adds** files you save; it does not read or
  browse your existing photos and videos.

## Third-party services
Quietly does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or data services.
The only third parties involved are (a) the servers of the links you choose to
open, and (b) the Google connectivity endpoint described above, each governed by
its own policies.

## Children
Quietly is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children. We do not
knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Security
Because Quietly keeps your data on your device and sends no personal data to us,
there is no server-side store of your information to breach. Downloads use a
secure (HTTPS) connection whenever the link you provide uses one.

## Your choices and deletion
- **Clear your data in-app:** use **Settings → Clear history** to remove your
  saved history. Removing a saved item deletes its local file.
- **Remove everything:** uninstalling the app deletes all app data from your
  device.
- Because we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to delete
  on our side, and no account to close.

## Your rights (GDPR / KVKK and similar laws)
We do not process personal data, so in practice there is little to exercise.
Where applicable law (including the EU/EEA GDPR and Turkey's KVKK) grants you
rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, you may contact us at
**hello@uselunexa.com** and we will respond as required. As all app data is
stored locally on your device, you remain in direct control of it at all times.

## International users
Quietly stores data locally on your device regardless of your location. We do
not transfer personal data internationally because we do not collect it.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected
here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after an update
means you accept the revised policy.

## Contact
Questions or requests: **hello@uselunexa.com**
